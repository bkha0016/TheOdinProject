A FRIES RECIPE

Description:

Make perfect FRIES at home with this classic homemade pizza recipe ,including a FRIES dough recipe, topping suggestions, and step-by-step insttructions with photos.

Ingredients:

For the FRIES dough

  • 1 1/2 cups warm water
  • 1 package active dry yeast
  • 3/4 cups bread flour
  • 2 tbps EVOO
  • 2 tbps kosher salt
  • 1 tbsp sugar

For the FRIES toppings

  • EVOO
  • Cornmeal
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Firm mozzarella cheese, grated
  • Fresh soft mozzarella cheese, separated into smalll clumps
  • Fontina/Parmersan/Feta chees, all grated
  • Pepperoni & Onions

Steps

  1. Proof the yeast
  2. Make & Knead the pizza dough
  3. Let the dough rise
  4. Preheat the pizza stone
  5. Divide the dough into 2 balls
  6. Prep the toppings
  7. Flatten the dough ball and stretch out into a round
  8. Brush the dough top with olive oil
  9. Spread with tomato sauce & sprinkle with toppings
  10. Slide pizza into the oven
